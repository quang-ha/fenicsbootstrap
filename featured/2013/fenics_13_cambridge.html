FEniCS‘13 at University of Cambridge, 18–19 March 2013¶

Featured article 2013-03-05

We are pleased to announce the 2013 edition of the annual FEniCS workshop:

FEniCS‘13: Expressive and high performance scientific computing

which will take place 18–19 March 2013 at Jesus College, Cambridge, UK. All FEniCS users, developers and other interested parties are invited to discuss current and future directions in scientific computing, approaches to scientific software development and FEniCS development.

Program¶

FEniCS‘13 Workshop Program

The workshop will run on 18 and 19 March.

Registration¶

Registration is now closed.

The registration fee includes lunch on both days of the workshop and a formal dinner at the historic Jesus College on the evening on 18 March.

Accommodation and travelling to Cambridge¶

A limited number of en-suite rooms at Jesus College have been reserved for workshop participants. Rooms can be booked here using the promotional code GW1803.

Information on reaching Jesus College can be found at http://www.jesus.cam.ac.uk/contacts-directions/travel-directions.

Contact¶

For information on the workshop, contact Garth N. Wells.
